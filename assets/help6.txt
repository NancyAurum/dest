Monsters are multiplying!  Attack them before they come after you!  Hints: 1. Build and upgrade a tower as soon as you can afford it.  It will help you locate enemy strongholds.  2. Attack with armies.  To create an army: A. Click on people in order to ready the ones who are not homed.  B. Attract the people to a nearby block.  C. Attract the army to an enemy stronghold.  See the "pyramids" section in the manual under the heading "moving units" for information about using the pyramids and the magnet bars to attract people to blocks.
